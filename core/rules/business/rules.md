# Business & Scoping Rules

These rules govern all product planning, scoping briefs, and business proposals in Liem OS.

## 1. Plan Before Build
- Before implementing any complex feature or system, draft a detailed **Product Requirements Document (PRD)** or a **Scoping Brief**.
- Ensure the plan is approved before writing implementation code.

## 2. Standard PRD / Brief Structure
Every business plan or PRD must contain:
1. **Executive Summary**: A 3-sentence description of the goal.
2. **User Stories & Requirements**: Functional and non-functional requirements.
3. **Out of Scope**: Explicit list of features or requirements excluded from this phase.
4. **Implementation Phases**: A step-by-step roadmap.
5. **Risks & Mitigation**: Technical, operational, and security risks with clear mitigation steps.

## 3. Assumption Transparency
- Clearly document all assumptions, constraints, and dependencies.
- State any financial, timeline, or resource limitations explicitly.
